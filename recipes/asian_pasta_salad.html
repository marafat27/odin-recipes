Asian Pasta Salad

Description

Asian Pasta Salad is full of flavor! The mixture of soy sauce and brown sugar add a wonderful salty sweetness and the veggies make it one delicious dish!

The flavors going on inside this pasta salad are so delicious. The mixture of soy sauce and brown sugar just take it to all levels of amazing.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Â¼ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped (Optional)
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas (Optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 10 to 12 minutes.
  2. Drain and rinse under cold water. Transfer pasta to a serving bowl and toss with olive oil.
  3. Whisk soy sauce, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, chili sauce, and sesame oil together in a bowl until sugar dissolves.
  4. Toss soy sauce mixture with pasta; top with green onions, red bell pepper, and snap peas. Refrigerate 30 minutes to overnight to allow flavors to blend.
  5. Toss it again before finally serving.
